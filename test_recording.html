Recording Diagnostic Test

Important Notes:

  • This records from your MICROPHONE only
  • Browsers cannot capture system audio or external speakers for security reasons
  • For doctor-patient conversations: Use a microphone placed between both people
  • Or use a conference microphone that picks up room audio
  • Make sure your microphone is not muted
  • Speak clearly and close to the microphone

Test Steps:

  1. Click "Start Recording"
  2. Allow microphone access when prompted
  3. Speak clearly INTO YOUR MICROPHONE for 5-10 seconds
  4. Watch the audio visualizer (should show waves)
  5. Click "Stop Recording"
  6. Check the results below
